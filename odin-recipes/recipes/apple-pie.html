Apple Pie

A splendid dessert

A tasty treat to enjoy after a meal.

Ingredients

  • Apples
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Pie Crust

Instructions

  1. Make the filling: On the stove, make a paste with flour and butter. Add the sugar and water and bring to a boil. Simmer, then remove from heat.
  2. Assemble the pie: Press one crust into a pie plate. Place the sliced apples on the bottom crust. Use the top crust to make a lattice crust. Pour the butter-sugar mixture over the lattice crust.
  3. Bake the pie: Bake the pie in a preheated oven until the apples are soft and the crust is golden brown.
